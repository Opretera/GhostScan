What is GhostScan?

Screenshares and client-side anti-cheats are the future of catching cheaters. Server-side anti-cheats eventually reach a point where they can no longer effectively detect all cheating methods, especially as cheat developers evolve. With GhostScan, we take a forensic approach, utilizing advanced techniques even used by experts in law enforcement, like the FBI, to thoroughly examine a player's PC.

By directly scanning the cheater's computer, GhostScan identifies cheat programs, injected code, and suspicious processes that server-side solutions can’t reach. It’s the most reliable way to ensure a player’s legitimacy, uncovering cheats like aimbots, auto-clickers, fly hacks, and more, even when they run outside the game.

Features of GhostScan

GhostScan offers a variety of features to help ensure fair play:

  • Comprehensive Cheat Detection: Detects every cheat program, injected code, or unauthorized software running on the player’s PC.
  • Real-time Monitoring: Monitors and identifies unauthorized programs or behavior in real-time, including aimbots, auto-clickers, and more.
  • Easy to Use: Simple interface for screen sharing, allowing server admins to quickly check for cheats on a player’s PC.
  • Works with 1.20+: Seamlessly integrates with Minecraft versions 1.20 and beyond.
